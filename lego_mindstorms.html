Lego Mindstorms

In this project we were divided into groups of five and given a Lego EV3 Mindstorms kit. The goal was to complete a series of assignments together as a team, and develop the soft skills involved with teamwork.

We initially divided ourselves into roles, with each member responsible for a different aspect of the project. We had a defacto group leader, responsible for arranging meetings and we had a secretary, who documented the process and meetings. During actual group hours, each member more or less contributed to every aspect of the process.

The communication skills and team building exercises employed here are something that I think will be very valuable going forward.
